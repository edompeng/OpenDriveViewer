# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We aim to provide a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Focus feedback on code and technical decisions, not people.
3. Assume good intent and collaborate in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
2. Trolling, insulting comments, or repeated disruptive behavior.
3. Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove or reject comments, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.
